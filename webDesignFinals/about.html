About us

Welcome to Burnelle Studio, where fashion meets a healthy disregard for the ordinary! Born from a collective obsession with threads, trends, and a dash of delirium, Burnelle Studio is your one-stop-shop for clothing that's as unique as it is questionably fashionable..

Our story

In the dimly lit confines of a basement-turned-studio (read: a glorified man-cave with sewing machines), Burnelle Studio came to life amidst a sea of discarded fabric scraps and way too many Pinterest fails. It all started with a sewing needle, a dream, and a group chat filled with emojis of dubious relevance.

From those humble beginnings, our ragtag team of fashion enthusiasts (and occasional sewing machine whisperers) has grown into a bona fide ragtag team of fashion enthusiasts who still occasionally whisper to sewing machines. Armed with caffeine and questionable taste in music playlists, we sew, we stitch, and we sometimes accidentally glue things together. It's a process, okay?

What Sets Us Apart

In a world where fast fashion reigns supreme and everyone's wardrobe seems eerily identical, Burnelle Studio proudly dances to the rhythm of our own slightly out-of-step sewing machine. Here's why we're the quirkiest thread in the fashion tapestry: Handcrafted with Love and a Little Bit of Sweat: Each garment is lovingly crafted in our not-so-secret basement lair. Sure, there may be a stray thread or two, but we like to call it "character". From Concept to Catwalk (or Couch): Our designs are inspired by everything from late-night Netflix binges to that one time we accidentally spilled coffee on a sketchbook. Expect the unexpected, but in a wearable form. Fashion Forward, Occasionally Sideways: We don't just follow trends; we create them. Or at least, we try really hard not to trip over them. Customer Satisfaction, One Stitch at a Time: We don't just sell clothes; we sell stories. Stories about that one time someone wore our dress to a wedding and got mistaken for the cake decorator. True story. So, whether you're a trendsetter or a trend-questioner, Burnelle Studio invites you to embrace your inner fashion misfit and join us on this slightly wonky journey through fabrics, caffeine-fueled brainstorming sessions, and occasional existential debates about the merits of polka dots versus stripes. Embrace the Weird, Wear the Wonderful, Welcome to Burnelle Studio!
